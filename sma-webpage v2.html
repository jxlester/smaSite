Accessibility and Inclusivity

The Student Marketing Association is committed to fostering an accessible, inclusive, and welcoming environment, recognizing the strength diversity brings to our community and the field of marketing. We ensure our website and digital content are accessible to all users.

Diversity

The Student Marketing Association fosters an inclusive community by embracing diversity across backgrounds, beliefs, and identities, ensuring everyone feels valued and respected. We achieve this through cultural guest speakers and promoting diverse representation in marketing events and materials.

Equity

The Student Marketing Association is committed to equity by ensuring fair treatment, access, and opportunities for all members. We support our members through resources, accommodations, and the removal of barriers to help them thrive and reach their full potential.

Inclusion

The Student Marketing Association prioritizes creating a welcoming and inclusive environment where every member feels heard, valued, and empowered to make an impact. Our leadership fosters belonging by encouraging participation in decision-making and building a strong sense of community.
